Help Me Diet, The Game

About

Our friend Uki wants to eat according to his diet and he needs your help, because he likes pizza too much. Help him by serving only healthy food!

Instructions

1 Behind one of the plate covers is a slice of pizza.
2 Your mission is to open all of the plate covers without running into pizza.
3 If you manage to uncover all the healthy food plates, Uki will be too full to eat from the last one - which is the slice of pizza and you win.
4 Good luck and have fun!
